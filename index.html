About Crescendo

"Crescendo is more than an organization. It's a family. A dedicated family to connecting generations through the entrancing power of music. Music is one of life's most beautiful creations. It brings joy and happiness. I created this organization to spread that happiness across the world. In this connected age, no one should feel lonely."

— Smyan Reddy, Founder and President

Our Mission

Crescendo exists to connect generations through live musical performances, bringing joy and happiness to communities in Suwanee, Georgia and beyond. We believe music has the power to heal, unite, and inspire people of all ages.

"Thank y'all so much for being there! I even noticed a Resident enjoying your music that doesn't come to any of my activities, so that is amazing!"

— Activity Director, Sunrise of Johns Creek
